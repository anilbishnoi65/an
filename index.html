Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any technical skills?

Absolutely not! DukaanLaunch is designed for everyone. If you can use a smartphone, you can build a beautiful online store with our platform.

What payment methods can I accept?

You can accept all major payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, etc.), Credit Cards, Debit Cards, and Net Banking.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes! On our Growth and Advanced plans, you can easily connect your own custom domain name (e.g., www.yourshop.in).

Our Story

DukaanLaunch was born in the vibrant streets of Jodhpur. We saw our friends, family, and countless talented artisans creating beautiful products but struggling to reach a wider audience beyond their local markets. The complexity and cost of existing e-commerce platforms were major barriers.

We decided to change that. Our mission is to provide a simple, affordable, and powerful platform that helps any Indian entrepreneur take their business online. We believe in the power of local commerce and are committed to building tools that help our merchants succeed.
